Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") explains how information is collected and used by GGtude Ltd. ("GGtude" or "we", "us", "our").

This Privacy Policy applies to GGtude's applications for mobile devices (the "App(s)"). By accepting this Privacy Policy, you give us informed consent to collect and process your personal data, and use it as explained in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting and respecting data privacy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

KEY POINTS OF THE PRIVACY POLICY

  • You may use the App either with or without registering as a user. Upon your initial use of the App you will be asked to provide your age and your preferred username. You may also, at your sole discretion, provide us your gender.
  • Users who wish to register, may do so by using their Apple, Google or Facebook account. Registering with one of these accounts will allow us to collect your account profile information.
  • We will also collect your Device Data and other general data.
  • We will store and process the information we collect about you for the purposes of providing you with our App, developing our App and conducting our research (using aggregated and de-identified information).
  • Your information may be handled by our Human Assistants and service providers for the purpose of providing support and maintaining the App.
  • We may process your personal information outside your jurisdiction. In such case, we will abide by data transfer rules applicable to these situations.
  • If you are an EU / UK resident, you have certain rights regarding your information.
  • We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time, and will inform you of such changes.

PERSONAL DATA PROCESSED

Initial Profile Information

We collect and process your age and your preferred username on your initial use of the App. You may also, at your discretion, provide us your gender.

Human Assistant Interaction Data

When you interact with our Human Assistants (HAs), we collect and process:

  • Communication data including messages and interaction timestamps
  • Feedback and interaction quality metrics
  • Progress data and self-assessments shared with HAs
  • Notes and documentation from HA interactions

Your Content

The App allows users to publicly share their statements or "thoughts" with other users, and to publicly share their progress and experiences through their social media accounts.

Registration Information

If you register through a Third Party Provider Account (Apple, Google, or Facebook), we may process your full name, email address and user profile photo.

Payment Related Information

When you submit a payment through App stores, we collect your user profile information and Account Manager information.

Device Data

We collect general data of your device, including country and Unique Device ID (UDID).

Analytics and Statistical Information

We collect usage data, interactions, self-assessments, and other statistical information to improve the App and conduct research.

DATA RETENTION AND RESEARCH USE

Research Data Processing and Storage

We process anonymized Statistical Information and Analytics Information for research purposes to study app efficacy and improve mental health interventions. This research data:

  • Is stripped of personally identifiable information
  • May be retained indefinitely for longitudinal research purposes
  • May be shared with academic research partners under strict confidentiality agreements
  • Is used to publish scientific papers and research findings without identifying individual users
  • Helps advance understanding of digital mental health interventions

General Data Retention

  • Active user data: Retained while your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: Data retained for 24 months after last activity
  • Human Assistant Interaction Data: Retained for 12 months after last interaction
  • Payment information: Retained as required by applicable laws
  • Usage logs and technical data: Retained for 6 months
  • Research data: Retained indefinitely in anonymized form

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting privacy@ggtude.com. However, anonymized data used for research cannot be deleted as it cannot be linked back to individual users.

HOW YOUR PERSONAL DATA IS USED

App Functionality

We process your information to provide core App functionality, track progress, and enable personalization.

Human Assistant Support

We use your Human Assistant Interaction Data to enable HAs to provide personalized support, monitor your progress, and escalate concerns when necessary. HAs have limited access to your information based on their role requirements and follow strict confidentiality protocols.

Research and Development

We use anonymized data to conduct research, improve app efficacy, and advance understanding of digital mental health interventions.

SECURITY MEASURES

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL and TSL encryption
  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

If we transfer your personal data for processing at locations outside your jurisdiction, we will abide by data transfer rules applicable to these situations.

MINORS

The App is not directed to users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes. Your continued use of the App after being notified of changes indicates acceptance of the new policy.
